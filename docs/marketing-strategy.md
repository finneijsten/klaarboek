# KlaarBoek — Marketing Strategy & Budget Plan

## Target Audience

### Primary: Dutch ZZP'ers (Freelancers)
- 1.2-1.6 million ZZP'ers in the Netherlands
- Pain points: bookkeeping is boring, expensive, and error-prone
- Most are tech-savvy but not financially trained
- Price-sensitive — many still use spreadsheets or basic tools

### Sub-segments (priority order)
1. **New ZZP'ers** (< 2 years) — don't have a system yet, easiest to convert
2. **Unhappy switchers** — using Moneybird/e-Boekhouden but frustrated with manual work
3. **Accountant-dependent** — paying €500-2000/yr for basic bookkeeping, ready to save

## Marketing Channels

### Organic (Free / Low Cost)

#### 1. SEO & Content Marketing
- Blog posts targeting high-intent keywords:
  - "BTW-aangifte ZZP 2026"
  - "beste boekhoudsoftware ZZP"
  - "boekhouding automatiseren freelancer"
  - "hoe BTW-aangifte doen als ZZP'er"
- How-to guides, tax tips, financial planning for freelancers
- Compare pages: "KlaarBoek vs Moneybird", "KlaarBoek vs e-Boekhouden"
- **Target: 20 articles in first 3 months**

#### 2. YouTube / Video Content
- Short tutorials: "BTW-aangifte in 5 minuten met KlaarBoek"
- Screen recordings of the product
- "Day in the life of a ZZP'er" content
- **Target: 2 videos per month**

#### 3. LinkedIn
- Founder posts about building KlaarBoek
- ZZP community engagement
- Thought leadership on freelancer finance
- **Target: 3 posts per week**

#### 4. Reddit / Online Communities
- r/Netherlands, r/freelance
- FOK! Forum, Tweakers Pricewatch
- ZZP Facebook groups (50+ groups, 100K+ members total)
- **Target: weekly community engagement**

### Partnerships (Low/No Cost)

#### 5. KvK (Kamer van Koophandel)
- Partner for starter events and workshops
- Get listed as recommended tool for new ZZP'ers
- Co-create content about financial administration

#### 6. ZZP Nederland (Association)
- Member benefit / discount partnership
- Newsletter placement
- Event sponsorship

#### 7. Accountant Referrals
- Accountants recommend KlaarBoek for clients who need basic bookkeeping
- Accountant portal (v2) as incentive
- Revenue share: accountant gets 20% commission for first year

#### 8. Freelance Platforms
- Freelance.nl partnership
- Headfirst, Brainnet, Yacht integration
- Banner placement or tool recommendation

### Paid Channels

#### 9. Google Ads
- Target high-intent keywords: "boekhoudsoftware ZZP", "BTW-aangifte tool"
- Estimated CPC: €1.50-3.00 for Dutch bookkeeping keywords
- Start small, scale what converts

#### 10. Facebook/Instagram Ads
- Target: ZZP'ers, freelancers, entrepreneurs in NL
- Lookalike audiences from waitlist signups
- Creative: short video demos, before/after comparisons

#### 11. LinkedIn Ads
- Sponsored content targeting self-employed professionals in NL
- Higher CPC (~€5-8) but more qualified leads

## Launch Plan

### Phase 1: Pre-Launch (Now - Q3 2026)
- Build waitlist via landing page
- Start SEO/content engine (blog, compare pages)
- LinkedIn founder journey posts
- KvK + ZZP Nederland outreach
- **Goal: 1,000 waitlist signups**

### Phase 2: Beta Launch (Q3 2026)
- Invite first 100 users from waitlist
- Collect feedback, iterate fast
- Case studies from beta users
- Press outreach: Sprout, MT/Sprout, Emerce
- **Goal: 500 active beta users**

### Phase 3: Public Launch (Q4 2026)
- Open registration
- Referral program: 1 month free per invite
- Google Ads campaign
- YouTube launch video
- Product Hunt launch
- **Goal: 5,000 users by end of Q4**

### Phase 4: Growth (2027)
- Scale paid ads based on CAC data
- Accountant partnership program
- Expand to Belgium (similar tax system)
- Mobile app launch
- **Goal: 40,000 users by end of 2027**

## Referral Program

- **Referrer gets:** 1 month free per successful referral
- **Referee gets:** First month free
- **Cap:** Max 12 free months per user (= 1 year free if you refer 12 people)
- **Tracking:** Unique referral link per user
